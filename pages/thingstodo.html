Pick Your Own Produce

There’s something magical about harvesting your own food. At Farm, you can stroll through our fields and pick seasonal fruits and vegetables straight from the earth. It’s a fun, hands-on way to connect with nature and taste the freshness of farm life.

Meet Our Friendly Animals

From playful goats to curious chickens, our animals are always excited to meet new friends. Visitors can feed them, learn about their care, and enjoy a close-up farm experience that’s both educational and heartwarming.
